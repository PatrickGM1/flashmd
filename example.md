# Chapter 1: Ancient History

## Who built the Great Pyramid of Giza?
The Egyptians, built for Pharaoh Khufu around 2560 BC.

## What was the Roman Empire at its peak?
The largest empire in the ancient world, spanning from Britain to Mesopotamia. Peak around 117 AD under Emperor Trajan.

## What caused the fall of the Western Roman Empire?
A combination of military pressure from Germanic tribes, economic troubles, political instability, and overextension.

# Chapter 2: Science Basics

## What is Newton's First Law?
An object at rest stays at rest, and an object in motion stays in motion, unless acted upon by an external force.

## What is the difference between mass and weight?
Mass is the amount of matter in an object (kg), constant everywhere. Weight is the gravitational force on that mass (N), varies by location.

## What is DNA?
Deoxyribonucleic acid — a molecule carrying genetic instructions for development, functioning, growth, and reproduction of all living organisms.

# Chapter 3: Programming

## What is the difference between a stack and a queue?
Stack: Last In First Out (LIFO) — push/pop from the same end. Queue: First In First Out (FIFO) — enqueue at back, dequeue from front.

## What is a deadlock?
A situation where two or more processes are each waiting for the other to release a resource, so none can proceed.

## What is the difference between TCP and UDP?
TCP: connection-oriented, reliable, ordered delivery. UDP: connectionless, no guarantees, lower overhead. Use TCP for accuracy, UDP for speed.
